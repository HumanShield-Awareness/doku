## What the platform does

<CardGrid>
	<Card title="Awareness training (LMS)" icon="open-book">
		<p><Badge text="New Feature" variant="tip" size="medium" /></p>
		Self-hosted **mandatory training with videos**: automatic assignment on a
		low awareness score, tamper-proof progress tracking, comprehension quizzes
		and audit-proof certificates. Set it up under
		[Training module (LMS)](/en/guides/schulungsmodul/).
	</Card>
	<Card title="Campaigns and tracking" icon="email">
		Phishing campaigns with scheduling, templates (HTML/Markdown, `.eml`
		import), landing pages and per-recipient evaluation – opens, clicks and
		form submissions. See [Features](/en/reference/funktionen/).
	</Card>
	<Card title="Security built in" icon="seti:lock">
		Argon2id passwords, secrets encrypted at rest, 2FA with backup codes,
		audit log and hardened containers. More under
		[Security](/en/reference/sicherheit/).
	</Card>
	<Card title="Self-hosted and vendor-neutral" icon="setting">
		Runs as a Docker Compose stack with any operator; all environment-specific
		values come from `.env` – nothing is hard-coded. Get going with the
		[installation](/en/guides/installation/).
	</Card>
	<Card title="NIS2 and BSI IT-Grundschutz" icon="approve-check">
		Measurable, repeatable awareness measures with evidence for audits – see
		[NIS2 and BSI](/en/reference/nis2-und-bsi/).
	</Card>
</CardGrid>
